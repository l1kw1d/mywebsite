Senior Cybersecurity Analyst

Securing Digital Assets & Infrastructure

Professional Summary

A seasoned cybersecurity professional with over 20 years of experience in technology and more than a decade in the cybersecurity industry. Possesses in-depth expertise in identifying, mitigating, and preventing cyber threats, while implementing robust security frameworks that ensure data confidentiality, integrity, and availability. Skilled in aligning security initiatives with business goals, fostering seamless collaboration with shareholders, business owners, and key stakeholders to drive innovation and ensure operational resilience.

Deep knowledge in SCADA and Operational Technology (OT) security, ensuring the protection of critical infrastructure and industrial control systems. Proven abilities in designing and securing complex architectures across cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, OpenStack(OVH)), data centers, and hybrid environments, leveraging IaC methodologies to streamline deployments and enhance system scalability.

Experienced in AI-driven threat detection and predictive analytics, employing advanced algorithms to preempt emerging threats and automate incident response processes. Adept in delivering secure solutions for diverse sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial clients, with a deep understanding of network protocols and secure communications within IoT ecosystems.

Highly proficient in governance frameworks, compliance audits, and risk assessments, ensuring organizations meet regulatory requirements while maintaining agility. Expertise in SOC operations, threat hunting, and security monitoring, with hands-on experience in deploying SIEMs and other cybersecurity tools. Dedicated to continuous learning, staying current with industry advancements, and leveraging emerging technologies to build innovative security solutions for a secure and sustainable future.

Leading innovation in Web3 and Fintech security, with extensive experience in securing blockchain networks, smart contracts, and DeFi protocols. Proven track record in implementing secure cryptocurrency trading platforms, NFT marketplaces, and decentralized applications (dApps). Expert in conducting smart contract audits, implementing secure wallet solutions, and protecting digital assets across multiple blockchain networks. Specialized in developing security frameworks for fintech applications, ensuring compliance with financial regulations while maintaining robust protection against emerging crypto-threats and financial fraud attempts.
